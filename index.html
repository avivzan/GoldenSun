What is Kombucha?

Kombucha is a fizzy, healthy, probiotic drink brewed from sweet tea and a SCOBY (Symbiotic Culture of Bacteria and Yeast), and fermented til the perfect balance of sweet, sour and naturally bubbly. A health tea culture passed down for generations, Kombucha was first recorded over 2000 years ago in Japan by a Korean scientist name Kombo. Drank by Samurais and Emperors, it traveled the Silk Road throughout Asia and Russia, and has been found for centuries in grandmothers' kitchens talk over Europe. Now Kombucha can be found in all corners of the globe as a fizzy fermented health drink balancing the gut bacteria of our world.

Our Story

Ashley and Ami did CouchSurfing with incredible women at a Kombucha house California. It is there they got inspired and learned how to make the amazing beverage, how to flavor and get just the right fizz, and about all the amazing health benefits of this magic elixir. When they moved to Israel, Ami got a tummy bug and the doctors found nothing wrong with him, so they decided to start making their own Kombucha from a SCOBY that Ashley brought back from the US - and within 2 weeks his gut was back to normal. They took this as a sign from the Universe to bring Kombucha to a land where it wasn't widely available, spread Kombucha to the people of Israel. They hosted tasting parties while brewing in their own kitchen, got orders for bottles from friends, and moved to a small brewing facility after maxing out the capacity of their home refrigerator. In May 2018 they opened the first Kombucha Bar in Israel - Golden Sun Cafe on Allenby Street in Tel Aviv.
